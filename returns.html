Returns & Refund Policy

Understand our return eligibility, refund process and important conditions before placing an order.

Last Updated 01 January 2026

Products may be eligible for return only if they satisfy the applicable return conditions specified by JK Enterprises.

Please contact our support team before returning any product and keep your order details ready.

Returned products should be unused, complete and in their original packaging with all accessories.

Some products may not be eligible for return because of hygiene, safety, manufacturer restrictions or other applicable conditions.

Report damaged or incorrect products promptly with photographs and your order details for faster assistance.

Returned items may be inspected before approval. Approved refunds are processed through the original payment method or another mutually agreed method.

Return shipping responsibility depends on the reason for return. Warranty claims may require assistance from the authorised service centre.
