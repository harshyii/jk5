Returns & Refund Policy

Learn about our return eligibility, refund process and important conditions.

Last Updated: 01 January 2026

1. Return Eligibility

Products may be eligible for return only if they meet the conditions specified by JK Enterprises.

2. Return Request

Customers should contact our support team before returning any product.

3. Condition of Returned Products

Returned products should be unused, complete and packed securely with all original accessories and packaging.

4. Non-Returnable Items

Certain products may not be eligible for return due to hygiene, safety, manufacturer restrictions or other applicable conditions.

5. Damaged or Incorrect Products

If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please notify us as soon as possible with relevant photographs and order details.

6. Inspection

Returned products may be inspected before approving a replacement or refund.

7. Refunds

Approved refunds will be processed through the original payment method or another agreed method where applicable.

8. Return Shipping

Responsibility for return shipping costs will depend on the reason for the return and applicable policy.

9. Warranty Claims

Products covered under manufacturer warranty may require service through the authorized service center.

10. Contact Support

If you have questions regarding returns or refunds, please contact JK Enterprises before sending any product.
