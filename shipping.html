Shipping Policy

Order processing, dispatch, delivery timelines and shipping information.

Last Updated: 01 January 2026

1. Order Processing

Orders are processed during business hours after payment confirmation and stock verification.

2. Dispatch

Orders are generally dispatched within the estimated processing time, subject to stock availability.

3. Delivery Coverage

We currently deliver across India. Delivery availability depends on the serviceability of your location.

4. Courier Partners

Orders are shipped through trusted courier and logistics partners selected according to destination and product type.

5. Shipping Charges

Applicable shipping charges are displayed during checkout before you place your order.

6. Delivery Time

Estimated delivery timelines vary depending on stock availability, destination and courier performance.

7. Delays

Delivery may occasionally be delayed because of weather, transport disruptions, public holidays or other unforeseen events.

8. Damaged Shipments

Please inspect your package upon delivery. If you receive a damaged parcel, contact us as soon as possible with supporting photographs.

9. Order Tracking

Tracking details will be shared after dispatch whenever available from the courier partner.

10. Contact

For any shipping-related questions, please contact JK Enterprises before or after placing your order.
