Shipping Policy

Information regarding order processing, dispatch and delivery.

Last Updated: 01 January 2026

1. Order Processing

Orders are processed during business hours after payment confirmation.

2. Dispatch Time

Orders are generally dispatched within the specified processing time, subject to stock availability.

3. Delivery Locations

We currently deliver across India. Delivery availability may vary depending on the destination.

4. Delivery Partners

Orders may be shipped through trusted courier and logistics partners.

5. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, are displayed during checkout before order confirmation.

6. Delivery Time

Estimated delivery times may vary based on product availability, courier service and destination.

7. Delayed Shipments

Unexpected delays caused by weather, transport disruptions or other circumstances may affect delivery timelines.

8. Damaged Package

If your package appears damaged at the time of delivery, please inspect it carefully and contact us immediately if there are any issues.

9. Order Tracking

Tracking details will be provided once your order has been dispatched, where applicable.

10. Contact Us

For any shipping-related queries, please contact our customer support team.
