# How to Choose the Right Angle Grinder

Angle grinders are among the most versatile power tools used in fabrication, construction, woodworking and metalworking.

Choosing the correct machine depends on the application, disc size, motor power and safety requirements.

---

## Common Disc Sizes

### 100 mm (4 inch)

Ideal for

- Home use
- DIY work
- Light fabrication
- Cutting tiles
- Small metal jobs

---

### 125 mm (5 inch)

Suitable for

- Workshops
- Stainless steel
- Sheet metal
- Frequent cutting

---

### 180 mm (7 inch)

Recommended for

- Heavy fabrication
- Industrial maintenance
- Structural steel

---

### 230 mm (9 inch)

Designed for

- Thick steel
- Concrete
- Stone cutting
- Construction projects

---

# Motor Power

Choose according to workload.

| Power | Application |
|-------|-------------|
| 650–850W | DIY |
| 900–1200W | Workshop |
| 1300–1800W | Industrial |
| 2000W+ | Heavy Duty |

---

# Variable Speed

Variable speed grinders are recommended for

- Polishing
- Sanding
- Rust removal
- Stainless steel finishing

---

# Safety Features

Look for

- Soft Start
- Restart Protection
- Spindle Lock
- Side Handle
- Wheel Guard
- Anti-Vibration Grip

---

# Recommended Accessories

- Cutting Disc
- Grinding Wheel
- Flap Disc
- Wire Cup Brush
- Diamond Blade

---

# Maintenance Tips

- Clean air vents regularly.
- Replace worn carbon brushes.
- Inspect the power cord.
- Never use damaged grinding wheels.
- Store in a dry location.
